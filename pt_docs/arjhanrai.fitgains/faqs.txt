Team Fit-Gains — FAQs

Do I have to cut out my favourite foods?
No. That's the whole point. Arjhan teaches you how to still eat the foods you enjoy — Indian food, takeaways, chocolate, alcohol — and make progress. Cutting everything out is not sustainable and is not the approach here.

How do check-ins work?
Daily check-ins are done through the coaching app. You log your training, nutrition, and a quick update. Arjhan reviews these and adjusts the plan based on what's working.

Do I need a gym membership?
No. Programmes are built around whatever equipment and environment you have — gym, home, or both.

How quickly will I see results?
It depends on your starting point, consistency, and goal. Arjhan will give you an honest expectation on the free call before you start. This is not a quick fix — but real, lasting change happens fast when you're consistent.

I've tried loads of diets before and nothing has worked. Can you help?
Yes — and this is exactly the kind of person Arjhan works best with. The reason most diets fail is they're too restrictive. Arjhan's approach is built around flexibility and education, which is why his clients actually stick to it.

Is this only for fat loss?
The main focus is fat loss and physique improvement, but the education around nutrition and training applies to any goal. If you're unsure whether it's right for you, book the free call and Arjhan will be straight with you.

How do I get started?
Book a free 30-minute call using the link in this chat. Arjhan will ask about your goals, your lifestyle, and your history — and tell you honestly whether he thinks he can help.
