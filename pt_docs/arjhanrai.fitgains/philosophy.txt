Team Fit-Gains — Coaching Philosophy

Arjhan has been working in the fitness industry since 2015 and has helped hundreds of people lose body fat and build muscle sustainably.

The core belief: you should not have to cut out the foods you enjoy to get results. Most people fail diets because they go too restrictive — they end up miserable, frustrated, and give up. Arjhan's approach is different.

Clients are taught how to still eat the foods they love — roti, chocolates, takeaways, a beer — and still make real progress toward their goals. This is achieved through flexible nutrition principles, calorie and macro education, and a step-by-step learning process.

The focus is on building knowledge, not dependency. By the end of coaching, clients understand how to eat more food while maintaining results — not just follow a plan blindly.

This isn't about suffering through a transformation. It's about building a body and a lifestyle you can sustain long-term.

Arjhan is a family man based in the UK with 2 young kids and 2 dogs. He coaches both UK-based and international clients and has travelled to India to hold seminars on health and fitness.

Who Arjhan works best with: people who have tried restrictive diets before and struggled to stick to them, people who want to enjoy food and social occasions while still making real progress, and people who want to be educated — not just handed a plan.
