FAQS

Do I need to follow a strict diet?
No. The whole philosophy is built around building a stronger relationship with food — not restriction. You'll learn how to manage hunger and cravings as part of the process, not fight against them with hacks and alternatives. The goal is flexibility and freedom with food long term.

I've tried losing weight before and it hasn't worked. Why would this be different?
That's the most normal thing to feel. If it hasn't stuck before, your brain has no reason to believe this time will be different — and that's okay. What makes this different isn't more discipline or more restriction. It's changing how you see the process — from something you're forcing yourself through to something you're choosing every day. That shift changes everything.

Do I need to give up my favourite foods?
No. The approach isn't about cutting things out. It's about building the daily habits that give you more freedom with food over time. People who seem to "eat what they want" have usually earned that flexibility through consistent daily habits — gym routine, step count, sleep, consistency. That's what we build together.

How long does it take to see results?
Results vary depending on where you're starting from and what your goals are. What I can tell you is that one good year of hard work, discomfort, and consistency is enough to instil habits that make this a lifestyle — not a diet you go on and off. The goal is always long term.

Is this only for people who want to lose weight?
The focus is fat loss, toning, and building a healthier relationship with food and exercise. If that's what you're looking for, we're probably a good fit.

Do you work with people who have tried lots of diets before?
Yes — in fact that's most of the people I work with. If you're exhausted from diet after diet and want something that actually lasts, that's exactly what this is built for.
