DISCOVERY CALL / APPLICATION

The first step to working with me is to apply at zachkieranfitness.com/apply.

The application takes a few minutes to fill in. It helps me understand where you're at, what your goals are, and whether we'd be a good fit to work together.

Once you've applied, we'll get on a call. This is a chance to talk through your goals properly, understand your history with food and exercise, and figure out exactly what working together would look like for you.

There's no hard sell. If it's not the right fit, I'll tell you. But if it is, we'll talk about next steps on the call.

The call is the best way to get real answers to your questions and understand what the coaching actually involves. Everything is personalised — so a call is the only way to give you an accurate picture of what to expect.

To get started: zachkieranfitness.com/apply
