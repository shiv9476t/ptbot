James Hartley — Coaching Philosophy

James doesn't believe in quick fixes. Every programme he writes is built around one question: what can this person actually stick to?

Sustainability over suffering. Most people fail not because they lack discipline but because their plan doesn't fit their life. James builds programmes around your schedule, your preferences, and your starting point — not a template.

Strength training is the foundation. Regardless of goal — fat loss, muscle gain, performance — James programmes resistance training as the core. Cardio is a tool, not a punishment.

Nutrition without obsession. James uses flexible dieting principles. No food is banned. Clients hit targets without eliminating the things they enjoy.

Honesty over hype. James won't promise you'll lose 10kg in 4 weeks. He will tell you exactly what's realistic, what it takes, and whether he thinks he can help you.

Who James works best with: people who are serious about making a change, have tried before without lasting results, and want a coach who will tell them the truth and hold them accountable.
