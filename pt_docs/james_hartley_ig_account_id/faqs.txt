James Hartley Fitness — FAQs

Do I need a gym membership?
No. James programmes for home, gym, or both depending on what you have access to.

How do check-ins work?
Daily check-ins are done through the coaching app (TrueCoach). Takes about 2 minutes — you log your training, nutrition, and a quick note on how you're feeling. James reviews every one.

How quickly will I see results?
Depends on the goal and starting point. Most clients notice meaningful changes within 4–6 weeks. James will give you an honest expectation on the discovery call based on your specific situation.

Do you work with complete beginners?
Yes. A significant portion of James's clients have never trained consistently before. The programme and the support are built around where you're starting from.

What if I travel or have a busy week?
The programme adapts. James builds flexibility in from the start and adjusts when life gets in the way. This is exactly what ongoing coaching is for.

Can I do this alongside my current training?
Depends on what you're currently doing. James will go through this on the discovery call and give you an honest answer.

How do I get started?
Book a free discovery call using the link James's assistant will send you. It's a 20-minute chat — no pressure, no sales pitch. James just wants to understand your situation and see if he can genuinely help.
