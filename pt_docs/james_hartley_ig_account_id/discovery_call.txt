James Hartley Fitness — Discovery Call

The discovery call is a free 20-minute video call with James.

What it's for:
James uses it to understand your goals, your situation, and whether he genuinely thinks he can help you. If he doesn't think coaching is right for you at this point, he'll tell you.

What to expect:
- James will ask about your goals, history, lifestyle, and what's held you back
- He'll explain how his coaching works and which option might suit you
- You'll have time to ask him anything
- No hard sell — if it's a fit, great. If not, James will point you in the right direction.

How to book:
Use the Calendly link provided in this chat. Pick a time that suits you.
