I'll be real — I don't have specific client transformation numbers to throw at you from what's shared here, but I can tell you my own story because I've lived this.

I started training at 17 years old, benching a plate for six reps. Pathetic. Over 11 years I went from that to pulling five and a half plate RDLs and six plate stiff-leg deadlifts. That foundation of heavy hinge work is honestly what built my physique.

I've gone through every phase you can imagine — CrossFit, powerlifting-style 5x5s, Renaissance Periodization mesocycles, N1 Nutrition sagittal plane work, Jordan Peters-influenced intensity training, push pull legs, push pull arms legs, upper lower, and now full body. I didn't just try these — I lived them, tracked them, and understood what worked and what didn't.

About 10 months ago I committed to full body training after being skeptical for years, and I fell in love with it. Understanding the physiology behind why it works for naturals changed the game.

I've been coaching online for roughly 6 years. The people who work with me stay because I bring genuine knowledge and daily study into their programming — not recycled templates.
