What's good — my name is Keenan Malloy. I'm 28 years old, born and raised in Vancouver, British Columbia. I've been into fitness my whole life — played soccer and football for about 20 years, which gave me a strong athletic foundation.

I started training at 17 through CrossFit with my mom and weight training in high school. I had a weightlifting coach named Johnny who taught me the fundamentals — deadlift, squat, bench, cleans, snatches, the works. That early foundation in movement was everything.

Over 11 years of training I've done it all — CrossFit, powerlifting-style programs, Renaissance Periodization, N1 Nutrition, Jordan Peters-influenced training, P90X, you name it. I bought JP's lifetime subscription and consumed every piece of content he ever put out. That era shaped how I think about training intensity and practical application of science.

I did two years of business in university, hated it, then switched to kinesiology for two years. Never finished the degree, but it gave me a strong academic foundation. The majority of what I know now comes from daily self-study — muscle physiology, nutrition, anatomy, exercise science. I study every single day. It's my biggest passion.

I've been doing online coaching for about 6 years. I'm not just a coach who hands out programs — I'm someone who genuinely lives this and brings real knowledge to every decision I make for my clients.
