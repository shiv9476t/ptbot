How does online coaching work?
You get fully customized programming and nutrition guidance delivered through an app. We check in regularly so I can see your progress, review training footage, adjust your program, and make sure everything's dialed in. It's not a PDF and a good luck — I'm actively coaching you.

What app do you use?
Your programming is delivered through a training app so you can track everything in real time. If you have questions about the specific platform when you sign up, just ask and I'll walk you through it.

How often do we check in?
We have regular structured check-ins to review everything — training, nutrition, recovery, how you're feeling. Between check-ins, I'm available for questions. Communication is a big part of how I coach.

Do you do in-person training or just online?
I'm based in Vancouver, BC, but my coaching is online. That means I can work with you no matter where you are in the world.

How long until I see results?
Depends on where you're starting and how consistent you are. Most people start noticing real changes within the first few weeks — better performance, better recovery, body composition shifts. This isn't a quick fix though, it's building something that lasts.

What happens if I travel or miss a week?
Life happens. We adjust. I can modify your programming around travel, schedule changes, whatever. The goal is sustainability — not perfection.

Do I need to be advanced to work with you?
Not at all. A lot of my clients do basic movements. I program based on where YOU are — whether you're just getting started or you've been training for years and want to optimize.

Do you handle nutrition too?
Yes. Nutrition support is part of the coaching. Whether that's full macro guidance or a more flexible approach depends on your goals and what's realistic for your lifestyle. I keep things practical, not overcomplicated.
