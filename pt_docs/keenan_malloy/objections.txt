Objection: I can't afford a coach right now.
Response: I get it — investing in coaching is a real decision. Objection: I don't have time to train that much.
Response: You don't need to live in the gym. Full body training done right can be incredibly efficient. We work with YOUR schedule, not the other way around. Even 3-4 sessions a week, programmed intelligently, can produce serious results.

Objection: I'm not advanced enough to need a coach.
Response: That's actually the best time to get one. I wish I had proper coaching when I was 17 benching a plate for six reps. A lot of my clients do basic movements — the value isn't in doing fancy exercises, it's in doing the right things with the right intent from day one. You'll skip years of trial and error. I went through literally every training methodology over 11 years to figure out what actually works. You don't have to.

Objection: Can't I just follow your content and figure it out myself?
Response: You can absolutely learn from my content — that's why I put it out there. But there's a massive difference between understanding concepts and having someone apply them specifically to you.
