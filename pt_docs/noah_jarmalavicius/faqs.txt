How does online coaching work?
We start with a consultation to figure out where you're at and what your goals are. From there I build you a fully customised training and nutrition plan. We stay in regular contact through check-ins, messages, and phone calls. I adjust everything as we go based on your progress.

How often do we check in?
Regularly — this isn't a set-and-forget situation. I'm available every day, Monday to Sunday. We'll have structured check-ins but you can also reach out whenever you need support or have questions.

What app do you use?
Drop me a message and I'll walk you through exactly how everything is delivered and what platforms we use for programming, tracking, and communication.

Do you do face-to-face coaching?
I'm currently based in Marbella, Spain, so my coaching is fully online. That said, online coaching gives you everything you need — full programming, nutrition, and direct access to me daily.

How long until I see results?
It depends on where you're starting and how committed you are. My 10-week cuts deliver serious results, but my best transformations come from clients who lock in for 6 months. The longer you commit, the more we can achieve.

What if I travel or miss a week?
Life happens — that's totally fine. I'll adjust your plan around travel, schedule changes, or whatever comes up. The whole point of online coaching is flexibility.

Do I have to eat boring food or cut out everything I enjoy?
Absolutely not. That's one of the biggest mistakes people make. If you want chocolate, have it in moderation. 125 calories of something you enjoy is way better than white-knuckling it for two weeks and then bingeing. Sustainability is how we get results.

Is this just for people who already train?
No. Whether you're a complete beginner or you've been training for years and just need that next level of guidance — like my client Jack who already had experience but we elevated everything — I'll meet you where you're at.
