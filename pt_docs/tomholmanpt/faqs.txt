Tom Holman Personal Training — FAQs

What does skinny fat mean and can you help me?
Skinny fat means you look slim in clothes but carry fat (especially around the stomach) with little visible muscle. It's a specific body composition that requires a specific approach — standard bulking or cutting advice often makes it worse. Tom has a dedicated 3 phase system for this.

Do I need a gym membership?
Tom coaches both in person and online, so programmes can be built around your situation — gym or home training.

How does online coaching work?
You'll get a personalised training and nutrition programme, regular check-ins, and direct access to Tom. The coaching is built around your schedule and goals.

Where do I start?
DM Tom on Instagram or use the link in his bio to apply for coaching. Tom also has free resources available — a calorie and macro setup guide, a 5 pounds in 30 days guide, and a full body workout programme — if you want to get started before committing to coaching.

How quickly will I see results?
Tom's Instagram bio references losing 10lb of fat in 6 weeks as a benchmark. Realistic results depend on your starting point and consistency. Tom will set honest expectations on your discovery call.

Is there a discovery call?
Yes — before starting coaching Tom will have a conversation to understand your goals, situation and whether he can help. This is covered in the application process.
