Tom Holman — Coaching Philosophy

Tom holds an MSc in Exercise Science and has been working as a personal trainer both in person and online. His approach is grounded in science, not bro culture.

Tom specialises in helping men — particularly those who are "skinny fat" (carrying fat while lacking muscle) — transform their physiques in a sustainable, educated way. His 3 phase system is built specifically for this body type because most generic bulk or cut advice doesn't apply.

Core beliefs:
- Remove the guesswork. Most people fail because they're following generic advice that doesn't apply to their situation. Tom builds programmes around the individual.
- Education over dependency. Tom teaches clients why they're doing what they're doing so they can maintain results long term.
- Fast track results. Tom's bio says "remove the guess work and fast track your results" — the aim is efficiency, not suffering through a plan that doesn't fit.
- Science-based. Tom's MSc background means his recommendations are grounded in evidence, not trends.

Who Tom works best with:
Men who are skinny fat, men who have tried generic programmes without results, men who want to understand training and nutrition properly, and men who are serious about building a better physique without wasting time on the wrong approach.
