How does online coaching work?
When you come on board, we'll jump on a call so I can understand exactly what you want to achieve. From there, I build your personalised training and nutrition plan, and we work together through structured phases. You'll submit weekly check-ins with photos, weight, and feedback, and I'll review everything and make adjustments to keep you progressing.

How often do we check in?
Weekly check-ins. Every week you'll send over your progress — photos, scale weight, how training's going, how you're feeling — and I'll go through everything in detail and update your plan as needed.

Do you do in-person sessions?
Yes, for local clients I do offer in-person training sessions and check-ins. I've trained with clients like Henry face-to-face, including getting final progress photos together.

What if I have social events, holidays, or a busy week?
That's exactly what I account for. I call this lifestyle coaching for a reason — we plan around work parties, drinks, travel, whatever's going on in your life. The goal is to still hit your targets without putting your life on hold.

How long until I see results?
It depends on where you're starting and what your goal is, but clients typically see significant changes within 12 weeks. Some transformations, like phased approaches, play out over several months for even bigger results.

Do I need to eat boring food or follow a strict meal plan?
Absolutely not. I'm big on making food enjoyable — proper seasoning, variety, and flexibility. There's no reason to eat bland chicken and rice. I'll show you how to hit your macros with meals you actually look forward to.

What happens after a cutting phase?
I don't just leave you hanging after a cut. We move into a structured reverse diet, drip-feeding carbs back in gradually to manage insulin sensitivity, get your body re-sensitized to more food, and set you up for your next phase — whether that's maintenance, gaining, or another goal.

What if I've been training for years but not seeing progress?
That's exactly the kind of person I work with. Like Gareth, who trained for a few years but was falling short of the next level. A structured, phase-based approach with proper coaching can unlock progress you didn't think was possible.
