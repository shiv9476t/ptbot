How does online coaching work?
Once you apply and we've had a conversation about your goals, I build out your fully personalised training and nutrition plan. Everything is delivered digitally so you can access it wherever you train. We stay in close communication throughout with regular check-ins, and I adjust everything in real time based on your progress, lifestyle, and feedback.

How often do we check in?
Communication is at the core of how we work. We maintain a high level of contact so I can pivot your plan when needed — whether that's around a social occasion, travel, or just how your body is responding. You'll never feel like you're left guessing.

How long are the coaching programmes?
We typically work in 12-week blocks, but the structure is tailored to you and your specific goals. Some clients stay on well beyond that initial phase as we continue building on their progress.

Do you offer in-person training?
Autonomy Coaching is an online service, which means you can work with me from anywhere in the world. Everything is structured so you can follow it in your own gym on your own schedule.

How long until I see results?
This depends on where you're starting from and how dialled in you are with execution. That said, clients like Sam and Kieron saw significant transformations within 12 weeks. When the plan is clear and you commit to the process, results come faster than you'd expect.

What if I travel or miss a week?
Life happens — that's exactly why our coaching is built around flexibility. We communicate closely and I adjust your plan to work around travel, social events, and anything else that comes up. That ability to pivot is one of the biggest advantages of working with us.

Do I need to be experienced in the gym to work with you?
Not at all. Whether you've been training for years or you're stuck and need a clear direction, the coaching is built around where you are right now. We clean everything up first and build from there.

What makes Autonomy Coaching different from other online coaches?
We don't just hand you a plan and disappear. This is a high-communication, high-accountability service where everything — training, nutrition, sleep, stress, mindset — is covered. I lead from the front, I practise what I preach, and the standards we set are world class. This isn't generic coaching.
