I'm Mark Byrne, head coach and founder of Autonomy Coaching.

The word 'autonomy' came into my world about three years ago on an educational course by Eric Helms. In his context, it described the ultimate goal of coaching — giving your client true independence and control. That idea stuck with me. Three years later, I brought it to life with this business.

I've spent years in the trenches — studying, coaching, and refining my approach to training and nutrition. I don't believe in coaching from the sidelines. I lead from the front. I hold myself to the same disgustingly high standards I expect from my clients, because if you're going to be an authority in this space, you have to walk the walk.

My coaching is built on evidence-based principles, meticulous planning, and a deep understanding of when to push and when to pull back. Those pivotal moments are where most people get it wrong, and that's where having the right guidance makes all the difference.

I built Autonomy Coaching because I was tired of seeing people overwhelmed by conflicting information and struggling to hit their goals. If you want clarity, structure, and someone in your corner who genuinely cares about getting you to your potential — that's what this is about.
