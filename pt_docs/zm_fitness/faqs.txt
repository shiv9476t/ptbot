How does online coaching work?
Once you're accepted, I set up your personalised training and nutrition plan based on your goals, experience, and lifestyle. You train on your own schedule, send me regular check-ins with progress pictures and weigh-ins, and I adjust your plan based on how your body is responding. Everything is done remotely so you can be anywhere in the world.

How often do we check in?
We do regular check-ins where you send me your progress — photos, weight, and how you're feeling. Based on that, I make any necessary adjustments to your training or nutrition so we're always moving in the right direction.

Do I need to go to a specific gym?
No, your program is designed around whatever gym or equipment you have access to. As long as you can train, we can make it work.

How long until I see results?
It depends on where you're starting and your goals, but most of my clients start seeing noticeable changes within the first few weeks. Bigger transformations typically happen over 8-16 weeks. Consistency is everything.

What if I slip up or fall off track?
It happens — and it's not the end of the world. One of my clients took a summer off and let himself go a bit, but he came back and we got him in the best shape of his life. Slipping up doesn't mean you've failed. It means you're human. We just bounce back and keep going.

Do you do in-person coaching?
Currently, my coaching is fully online. This means I can work with clients anywhere in the world and you get the flexibility to train on your own schedule.

How do I sign up?
Fill out the coaching application form in my bio. I'll review it and get back to you. Spots are limited so if you're serious, don't wait around.

Do you help with nutrition or just training?
Both. You get a fully personalised nutrition plan alongside your training program. I tailor everything based on whether we're bulking, cutting, or maintaining — and I adjust it as we go based on your check-ins.

What if I don't know whether to bulk or cut?
That's literally what I'm here for. Based on your current physique, body composition, and goals, I'll tell you exactly what phase we should start with and map out the plan from there. No more spinning your wheels.
