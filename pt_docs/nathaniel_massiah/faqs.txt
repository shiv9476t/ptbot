How does online coaching work?
You'll get your programming delivered through an app, and we stay in regular contact through check-ins. I review your training, look at your lift videos, adjust things as needed, and make sure nutrition is dialled in. It's a proper working relationship, not just a spreadsheet sent your way.

How often do we check in?
We do regular check-ins to go over how training is going, what's moving well, what needs adjusting. If something comes up between check-ins, you can always reach out — I'm not going to leave you hanging.

Do you do in-person coaching?
Right now it's online coaching, which honestly works brilliantly because I can work with you regardless of where you are. The programming, video reviews, and communication all happen remotely.

How long until I see results?
It depends where you're starting from, but with my client Montel we saw 80 kilos on his total in 9 months. If you're consistent, fuelling properly, and we make the right changes, you should start seeing progress within the first block or two.

What if I travel or miss a session?
Life happens — I get that. We adjust around travel, schedule changes, whatever comes up. The programming can be flexible. The key is communication so I can plan around it.

Do I need to follow a strict diet?
I'm not going to have you eating like a monk. But you do need to be getting enough protein, fuelling your sessions, and covering the basics — creatine, vitamin D if you need it, enough micros. It's about making the right decisions more often than not.

Is this just for powerlifters?
My background and expertise is in powerlifting, but the principles — progressive overload, proper nutrition, making smart changes when you plateau — apply to anyone serious about getting stronger. If strength is your goal, we'll work well together.

What makes you different from other coaches?
I practice what I preach at the highest level. I compete nationally and internationally as a natural lifter, and I've been doing this for 10 years. I'm not just giving you theory — I'm applying these exact principles to my own training and seeing results block after block.
