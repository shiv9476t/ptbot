What's up, I'm Messiah — most people know me as Nate. I've been in the gym since 2015, so we're at 10 years now, and I've been competing as an elite-level powerlifter for a couple of years. I've competed at Junior World Championships, British Open Nationals, European Championships — the big ones. I'm currently coached by Owen, and I'm a massive advocate for having a coach because I've seen firsthand what it does.

I got into coaching because I genuinely believe that most people who feel stuck just need someone to look at their training objectively and make the right changes. I've lived through plateaus myself — my bench stalled and I had to go back to the drawing board, change my form, change my split, change the exercises. That experience of problem-solving my own training is exactly what I bring to my clients.

I'm a natural lifter and I'm proud of that. Everything I've achieved has been through consistency, proper nutrition, smart programming, and being honest with myself about what I need to improve. I compete at 105 kilos, I've been as high as a 330 squat, 215+ bench, and 370 deadlift in competition. I work with my protein as a sponsored athlete, and I'm serious about showing that natural lifters can keep making progress year after year.
